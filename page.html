Sucker Lake, Kawartha Highlands

Sucker Lake is located in the Kawartha Highlands Provincial Park.

In order to get to Sucker Lake, you must first go to the access point on Bottle Lake.

After paddling across Bottle Lake, there is a short portage of ~200m to get to Sucker Lake, as displayed in the upper right map.
